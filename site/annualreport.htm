Annual Reports of TRAIC

Academic Session 2012-2013

  • Student Coordinator Of the Session was Hemant Kumar Suman AEI Deptt.
  • This year kicked off extremely well with an Exhibition held on 17/9/2012 with projects made by the second and third year students. This year a total of 35 trainees were enrolled and there was an exceptional rise in the total number of sessions of the trainees. Apart from the theoretical sessions, practical sessions were also held at the T.R.A.I.C Lab and an event on constructing a basic manual robot was held at the end of the odd semester which was a huge success and more than 40 students participated in the event.
  • Shashank Kumar (EN-2ND YEAR), Awik Sinha (ET-2ND YEAR), Sandeep Saurav (ET-2ND YEAR) and Vikrant Negi (ET-2ND YEAR) stood 1st in the line follower contest at TechSpardha’11, the annual technical fest of Meerut Institute of Technology, Meerut. This was for the 2nd consecutive time that members of T.R.A.I.C had won the competition.
  • Pushkar Shukla (ET-3RD YEAR), Hitesh Tripathi (ET-3RD YEAR), Shailesh Bisht(ET-3RD YEAR) and Lokesh Lohani (ME -2ND YEAR) stood 2nd in inter college level exhibition held at J.B. Institute of Technology out of 89 teams.
  • Pushkar Shukla (ET-3RD YEAR), Hitesh Tripathi (ET-3RD YEAR), Shailesh Bisht(ET-3RD YEAR) stood 1st in Dynamo gauge at Neuron’12, the annual technical fest of NIT Jaipur. The participants were asked to design a contact less tachometer using self-made modules.
  • Varun Joshi (ME-2ND YEAR) created a RC Controlled Aeroplane which was able to complete a successful flight. This project inspired other members of the club to work on aerodynamics. The project was presented at the exhibition held on 17/9/12.
  • Shailesh Bisht (ET- 3RD YEAR) designed 3*3*3 Led Cube used for 3-D display. The project was presented at the exhibition held on 17/9/12.
  • Pushkar Shukla (ET- 3RD YEAR) did a project on “Detection of shape drawn by human finger using a Camera”. A human finger was covered with a strap and a camera was used a sensor for detecting the position of the strap.

Academic Session 2011-2012

  • Student Coordinator Of the Session was Bhawesh Pandey EN Deptt.
  • This year might be defined as the year of transition .Apart from participating in different technical fests the members of The Robotics And Innovations Club took a leap forward and started working on various projects. A greater number of classes followed by an event at the end of the odd semester were the key highlights of the club this year.
  • Avi Srivastava (CS-4TH YEAR) published a research paper on A Hybrid Method for Analysis of Foot Pressure Image of Diabetic Mellitus Subject under the guidance of Dr.Kuldeep Yadav . The paper was submitted in IET Electronics Letter.
  • Pushkar Shukla (ET-2nd YEAR), Hitesh Tripathi (ET-2ND YEAR) and Lokesh Lohani (ME -2ND YEAR) stood 1st in the line follower contest at TechSpardha’11, the annual technical fest of Meerut Institute of Technology, Meerut.
  • Shailesh Bisht (ET-2nd YEAR), Gaurav Bhatt(CS- 2nd YEAR), Shubham Agarwal(AEI-2ND YEAR), Abhishek Bhatt (IT- 2ND YEAR) stood first in Grafest’11 , the annual techno-cultural fest of Graphic Era University in the line following event.
  • Avi Srivastava (CS-4TH YEAR), Ambuj Singh (AEI 3rd YEAR), Tushar Semwal (ET-3RD YEAR), Sanyam Gupta (ET-3RD YEAR), Ankit Kumar (ET – 3RD YEAR), Hemant Kumar ( AEI-3RD YEAR) and Pankaj Adhikari(CS-3rd year) did a project on I.C.U security system. The system was a prototype of a door whose movement was controlled by a finger print database.
  • Tushar Semwal (ET-3RD YEAR) did a project entitled “voice recognition based security systems using HM-2007”.
  • Tushar Semwal (ET-3RD YEAR) interfaced a mouse and a microcontroller and hence was successfully able to build a mouse controlled robot.
  • Pushkar Shukla (ET-2nd YEAR) did a project on “Gesture based machine control via image processing” where the motion of a robot was controlled by human fingers that had coloured straps on them which were sensed by a camera.

Academic Session 2010-2011

  • Student Coordinator Of the Session was Vatsalya Sharma ME Deptt.
  • During this session The Robotics and Innovations Club started to function as a body on the whole. It started training students of the college and provided them with the basic knowledge of Robotics. Proper lectures were arranged for the students. The club was also given the organising Robosync, the Robotics Event at Manthan, the annual technical fest of College of Engineering Roorkee.
  • A workshop on basic line following was conducted by Ambuj Singh (AEI- 2ND YEAR) ,Pankaj Adhikari (CS -2ND YEAR) and Ankit Kumar( ET-2ND YEAR).
  • Milind Agarwal (ET-3RD YEAR) , Dhruv Sharda (EN-3RD YEAR) and Bhawesh Pande (EN-3RD YEAR) organised a workshop on Swarm Robotics and Wireless Communications.
  • Avi Srivastava (3rd Year CS), Dhruv Sharda (3rd Year EN), Milind Agarwal (3rd Year ET) stood 1st in the North Zone of National line following Contest, Nexus, IIT Bombay held at Thapar University, Patiala.
  • Avi Srivastava (CS- 3rd YEAR) published research papers on the following topics
    • GPU parallel implementation of Colour based Medical Image retrieval in compressed domain.
    • Parallel implementation of shape based Image Retrieval on CUDA in compressed domain.
    • Parallel Implementation of texture based compressed domain using CUDA.
  • The article was published under the guidance of Dr.Ankush Mittal in the International Journal of computer application special issue on “Novel aspect of digital imaging” DIA (ISBN 978-93-80865-47-9).
  • Avi Srivastava published a research paper on Hybrid Content Based Medical Image Retrieval in Compressed Domain using Compressive Sensing under the guidance of Dr. Ankush Mittal. The paper was submitted in IEEE JETCAS special issue on circuit, system and algorithm in compressive sensing.
