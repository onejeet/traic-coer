:: The Robotics And Innovation Club ::
  • The Robotics And Innovation club was established (officially) in January 2010 in College Of Engineering Roorkee.

  • The Titans, the team for Robocon from College of Engineering Roorkee was formed on Sept.15,2007, by handful of budding first yearlings.

  • The long journey of officialising the club started in 2008-09 but it was until Jan. 2010, that the official recognition was granted.

  • The values which form the base of the club are Honour, Honesty and Sacrifice.
