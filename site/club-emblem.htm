    "Emblem of the club"



  • The emblem of the club is basically a symbol of traditions and work culture present in the club.

  • The emblem has the name of the club on the top, it’s motto on the bottom. The central part of the emblem is an integration of the three fields required in robotics that is mechanical, electronics and programming.

  • The gear and the hammer represent the mechanical wing of robotics. The mechanical section of robotics deals with the structural designing of the robot and the statics and dynamics of the robot.

  • The IC and traces are symbolic of the electronic section.Electronics not only connects different parts of a robot but they are also responsible for giving senses to a robot. It deals with designing sensors for a robot, making connections between different parts, designing of the development boards which act as the brain of the robot.

  • The binary digits represent the programming section. Programming for robots is generally done on a microcontroller. This type of programming is referred to as embedded programming. The microcontroller is the brain of a robot and it is programmed through soft wares. The programming decides the output of the robot under specified conditions.

  • The logo emphasises on team work and co-operation and that robotics is incomplete without the presence of any of the three branches.


    • "Motto Of The Club"

    "Honour-Honesty-Sacrifice"

  • The values which form the base of the club are Honour, Honesty and Sacrifice.

  • Honour forms the integral part of every TRAIC member. We take pride in our work and hence we command the honour of our fellow mates. We look forward for infusing such honour in all our fresh entrants every year. Every member who has the zeal, passion and dedication will always be honoured by the fellow members. We have and will always respect our seniors because of whom we have developed a technical attitude towards things.

  • Honesty is the best policy is an age old saying and thus we follow the same. We seriously discourage plagiarism in any form in our club. All our projects and work are an original piece of work which we don’t encourage to reproduce in any form. Honesty also implies being honest to oneself, your fellow team mates and most importantly to your work.

  • Sacrifice, For a great goal, you have to sacrifice many smaller pleasures. Thus, every TRAIC member is fully devoted to his work and the cause of the club on a whole. We encourage making the sacrifices which will become the ladders for our ultimate success..
